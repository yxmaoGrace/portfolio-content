Yixuan Mao

Hi Everyone! This is Yixuan, a loyal spicy lover since early childhood. Growing up in Lanzhou, China, a city in northwestern China with a population of different ethnicities, I got the chance to taste food with diverse flavors. With all kinds of food using different spices, ingredients, and cooking methods, my all time favorite flavor is SPICINESS.

For me, spiciness is a strong, pungent flavor that always brings excitement and comfort. It is a flavor that stimulates my taste buds and makes any meal less insipid and boring. Adding a little spoon of pepper oil, or adding several slices of peppers into a dish expands the layers of flavor of that dish, which offers a chance of being surprised with an extra excitement while enjoying a meal. Moreover, spicy food accompanied me through many low-spirited days by warming up my mouth and stomach. The strong flavor of spiciness can help me to relax for a moment and gather myself together to move forward.
